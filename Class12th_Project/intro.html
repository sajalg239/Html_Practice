Student Management System

Student Management System (SMS) streamlines data collection,storage & retrieval and the management of records to improve both operational (admission, registration, scheduling, examination and grading) and management tasks (planning, evaluation and decision-making).

SMS focuses on storing & processing curriculum data, student data and produce outputs supporting the operational activities. These outputs include student cards, subject descriptions, invoices, schedules and management information.

SMS directly integrates with the university’s finance department by effectively managing invoicing, receipting, sponsor management and other billing features.
